Smart Recycling Bin

Category: Engineering Design Year: 2024

Overview

This project was a part of a Make-a-thon where we were given about 48 hours with a prompt relating to sustainable solutions around campus. My idea was a bin that provided feedback on the usage it was getting. The more people recycled the more the ticker would go up. The goal was to encourage people to recycle more and to make it a fun and interactive experience.
