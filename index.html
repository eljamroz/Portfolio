About Me

Hello, I'm Ethan Jamroz, an aerospace engineering student with a passion for rocket propulsion. I am a senior at North Carolina State University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Aerospace Engineering. I will be graduating in May of 2026 and am currently seeking internship opportunities for the summer of 2025.

My experience includes work on propulsion systems, including a 31kN HTP/Jet-A bipropellant engine and a small Rotating Detonation Engine(RDE). I enjoy working on projects that challenge me to think creatively and solve complex problems. I'm passionate about sustainable propulsion engineering and am continually seeking opportunities to apply my skills to meaningful projects.

I also enjoy working on side projects, such as the smart recycling bin project featured in my portfolio. Apart from my engineering projects, I love to travel, rock climb, play soccer, scuba dive, do photography, and aquascape complex self-sustaining aquariums.
